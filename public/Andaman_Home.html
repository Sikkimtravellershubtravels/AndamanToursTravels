The Andaman and Nicobar Islands, located in the east of the Indian mainland geographically, and residing in the Bay of Bengal form the Andaman and Nicobar Islands Union Territory of India. These picturesque undulating islands are covered with dense damp and evergreen forests .Andaman is known for its marine life and endless varieties of exotic flora and fauna. These islands consist of many historically significant places to visit like Cellular Jail, Ross Island, Viper Island, Hopetown and Mount Harriet. Apart from these islands, there are many other island which you can plan and visit like Neil Island and Havelock island. Havelock Island is a popular destination in Andaman Islands. It is named after Henry Havelock. Mahatma Gandhi Marine National Park is the best known sanctuary here. These islands are blessed with the bounties of both south-west and north-east monsoons. There are many activities to enjoy here in the water bodies like scuba diving, snorkeling, sea-walking, glass bottom water boat,etc. So plan your holiday or honeymoon and enjoy the natural scenic beauty here.
